# Jupoly
Jupoly is a terminal-based polyrhythm visualizer written in Haskell.

## Usage
```
./make.sh
./jupoly 4 3 # for example
```

## Examples
```
$ ./jupoly 4 3
.  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .
a        a        a        a      
b           b           b         
.  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .
```
```
$ ./jupoly 2 3
.  .  .  .  .  .
a        a      
b     b     b   
.  .  .  .  .  .
```
```
$ ./jupoly 3 5
.  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .  .

b        b        b        b        b
```
